4. REFUND POLICY

4.1 General Principle:

All in-app purchases made through the Application are processed by the applicable Platform Provider (Apple Inc. or Google LLC). As a general rule, all in-app purchases are final and non-refundable once the digital content has been accessed or the premium feature has been delivered.

Under Article 15-3(1)(ii) of the Act on Specified Commercial Transactions, the statutory withdrawal right that applies to certain mail-order transactions does not apply to digital content where use of the content has commenced with the user's prior consent to the forfeiture of the withdrawal right. By initiating any in-app purchase, you expressly consent to the immediate delivery of the digital content and acknowledge that the right of withdrawal is therefore forfeited upon delivery.

4.2 Platform-Level Refunds:

Refund requests for in-app purchases must be directed to the applicable Platform Provider through their respective support channels:

  • Apple App Store: Refund requests can be submitted through Apple's report a problem page at reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple's refund decisions are made at Apple's sole discretion pursuant to Apple's Media Services Terms and Conditions.
  • Google Play Store: Refund requests can be submitted through Google Play's support at play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory. Google's refund decisions are made pursuant to Google Play's refund policy.

We do not process or approve refunds directly. Any refund approved by a Platform Provider will be made to the payment method on file with that Platform Provider.

4.3 Exceptional Circumstances — Our Goodwill Policy:

Notwithstanding the above, we may, at our sole discretion and without creating any obligation or precedent, consider refund or credit requests submitted to us directly in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • A verifiable technical defect in the Application prevented access to purchased premium features for the entire purchase period despite reasonable attempts to resolve the issue through our support channel.
  • A duplicate charge occurred due to a verified Application or Platform error.
  • The purchase was made fraudulently without your authorization.

Goodwill refund requests must be submitted to caloriesbunnysupport@gmail.com within 7 days of the original purchase date, with a clear description of the issue and relevant transaction details. We will review all requests in good faith but reserve the right to decline any request that does not meet these criteria.
