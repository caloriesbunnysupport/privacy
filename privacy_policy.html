3. CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED

We collect the following categories of personal information from users of the Application:

3.1 Registration and Account Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted/hashed form)

3.2 Health and Biometric Profile Data:

  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Body weight
  • Height
  • Activity level

Under the APPI, health-related personal information is treated as requiring heightened protection. We apply additional security and access controls to this category of data. We collect this information only with your explicit consent and solely for the purpose of providing personalized nutritional analysis and progress tracking within the Application.

3.3 Food and Nutritional Data:

  • Food photographs uploaded by users for AI nutritional analysis
  • Text-based food descriptions entered by users
  • Dietary logs and nutritional intake history

3.4 Technical and Device Data:

  • Device identifiers (as collected by Sentry for crash reporting purposes)
  • IP addresses and coarse network metadata (as logged by server infrastructure and Sentry)
  • Application version and operating system information
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • Session and authentication tokens

3.5 Usage and Analytics Data:

  • Feature usage patterns and interaction logs
  • In-app purchase transaction records

We do not collect precise geolocation data. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information beyond Health Data as described above.

4. HOW WE COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION

We collect personal information through the following means:

  • Directly from you when you register an account, complete your profile, or interact with features of the Application.
  • Automatically through the Application's technical infrastructure, including server logs, session management systems powered by Upstash, and error monitoring through Sentry.
  • Through the Google Gemini API when you submit food photographs or text descriptions for AI nutritional analysis. Please note that data submitted to Google Gemini is processed by Google's infrastructure and subject to Google's data processing terms.
  • Through Supabase, which serves as our backend database and authentication provider and processes data on our behalf under a data processing arrangement.

We do not collect personal information from third-party data brokers or social media platforms.

5. LEGAL BASIS AND PURPOSE OF DATA PROCESSING

Under the APPI and applicable PPC guidelines, we process your personal information on the following bases and for the following purposes:

  • Contractual Necessity: To create and manage your user account, provide the AI nutritional analysis services you have requested, process in-app purchases, and deliver the core functionality of the Application.
  • Consent: To collect and process Health Data including body weight, height, activity level, and date of birth for the purpose of generating personalized nutritional insights. You provide this consent at the time of profile creation and may withdraw it at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests: To maintain the security, stability, and performance of the Application through crash reporting and diagnostic monitoring via Sentry; to improve the Application based on aggregated usage analytics; and to protect against unauthorized use, fraud, and abuse.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable Japanese law, including obligations under the APPI, the Act on Specified Commercial Transactions, and any orders or requirements of Japanese regulatory authorities.

We do not use your personal information for automated individual decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without human review.

6. USE OF FOOD PHOTOGRAPHS AND AI PROCESSING

When you submit a food photograph or text description through the Application for nutritional analysis, the data is transmitted to the Google Gemini API for AI-powered processing. We wish to be fully transparent about the following:

  • Food photographs submitted for analysis are transmitted to Google's servers for processing by the Gemini AI model. We do not store original food photographs on our own servers beyond the period necessary to transmit them to the Gemini API, unless you explicitly save an entry to your dietary log.
  • The nutritional analysis results returned by the Gemini API are stored in your dietary log on our Supabase database infrastructure if you choose to save them.
  • Google's processing of data submitted through the Gemini API is governed by Google's API Terms of Service and applicable data processing agreements between us and Google.

We do not use your food photographs to train AI models without your explicit consent. We do not share your food photographs with third parties other than Google (via the Gemini API) in the course of providing the nutritional analysis service.

7. SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We share your personal information only in the following circumstances:

7.1 Service Providers and Data Processors:

  • Supabase (Supabase, Inc.): Database and authentication services. Processes your account data, health profile data, and dietary logs on our behalf.
  • Google LLC (Gemini API): AI nutritional analysis processing. Receives food photograph and text data for analysis purposes.
  • Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.): Error monitoring. May receive device identifiers, IP addresses, and technical metadata for crash reporting.
  • Upstash: In-memory caching and session management. May process session data to maintain application state.
  • Apple Inc. / Google LLC (Platform Providers): In-app purchase transaction processing.

All third-party service providers are engaged under appropriate contractual arrangements requiring them to protect your personal information in accordance with applicable law and to use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

7.2 Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your personal information where required to do so by law, court order, or in response to a legitimate request by a Japanese governmental or regulatory authority, including the PPC, or as otherwise required under applicable law.

7.3 Business Transfers:

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity as part of that transaction. We will provide notice of any such transfer and the opportunity to exercise your rights under the APPI.

8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

The Application integrates with services operated by companies located outside Japan, including Supabase (United States), Google LLC (United States), Sentry (United States), and Upstash (United States). When your personal information is transferred to these providers, it may be processed and stored outside Japan.

Such cross-border transfers of personal data are conducted in accordance with Article 24 of the APPI and the PPC's guidelines on the provision of personal information to third parties in foreign countries. We take the following measures to ensure appropriate protection:

  • We enter into data processing agreements with each third-party provider that impose obligations equivalent to those under the APPI.
  • We select service providers that maintain data centers in jurisdictions recognized as having adequate personal information protection frameworks, or that have implemented contractual safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

You may request information about the safeguards in place for cross-border transfers by contacting us using the contact details provided in Section 1.

9. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for the periods necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, subject to any longer retention periods required by applicable law.

  • Account and profile data: Retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 30 days of account deletion, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Dietary logs and nutritional analysis records: Retained for the duration of your account. You may delete individual records through the Application at any time.
  • Health profile data: Retained for the duration of your account and deleted promptly upon account deletion.
  • Technical and diagnostic data (Sentry crash reports): Retained for up to 90 days in accordance with Sentry's standard data retention configuration.
  • Transaction records: Retained for the period required under Japanese tax and commercial law, typically 7 years.

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, personal information will be securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with our data destruction procedures.

10. SECURITY MEASURES

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, destruction, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocols.
  • Secure storage of passwords using industry-standard hashing algorithms through Supabase's authentication infrastructure.
  • Role-based access controls limiting access to personal information to personnel who require access to perform their job functions.
  • Error monitoring and security alerting through Sentry to detect and respond to application anomalies.
  • Regular review of security practices in accordance with PPC security management guidelines (安全管理措置).

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information and cannot be responsible for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to safeguard your account credentials.

11. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER THE APPI

Under the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI), as amended by the 2022 reforms which took effect on April 1, 2022, you have the following rights with respect to your personal information held by us:

  • Right of Access (開示請求): You have the right to request disclosure of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right of Correction (訂正請求): You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.
  • Right of Deletion (利用停止・消去請求): You have the right to request deletion of your personal information where we no longer have a lawful basis for retaining it.
  • Right to Suspend Use (利用停止請求): You have the right to request that we suspend use of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Opt Out of Third-Party Transfers (第三者提供の停止請求): You have the right to request that we cease transferring your personal information to third parties in circumstances not required by law.
  • Right to Receive Explanation: Where we have made decisions affecting you based on automated processing, you have the right to request an explanation of such decisions and the right to request human review.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 1, clearly identifying yourself and describing your request. We will verify your identity before processing any request. We will respond within the timeframes required by the APPI.

If you are dissatisfied with our response to any privacy-related request or complaint, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Personal Information Protection Commission (個人情報保護委員会) at https://www.ppc.go.jp/.

We do not use automated processing to make decisions about you that produce significant legal or similarly significant effects. All outputs of the AI nutritional analysis feature are informational only and are not used to make automated decisions about your eligibility for services, your health status, or any other matter affecting your rights or interests.
