10. In-App Purchases and Payment Data

12. Contact Us

Cookie and Tracking Technologies Policy

CaloriesBunny (モグダイエット)

Effective Date: April 12, 2025
Last Updated: April 12, 2025

This Cookie and Tracking Technologies Policy explains how the Application uses session tokens, device identifiers, server-side logging, and similar tracking technologies. This Policy forms part of and is incorporated into our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.

1. What Are Tracking Technologies?

In the context of a mobile application, tracking technologies include technical mechanisms used to identify a device, maintain session state, collect usage data, or monitor application performance.

  • Session Tokens
  • Device Identifiers
  • Server Logs
  • Caching Identifiers

2. Types of Tracking Technologies Used

Strictly Necessary / Functional Technologies

  • Authentication tokens issued by Supabase upon login
  • Session cache keys used by Upstash
  • API request identifiers used to maintain data integrity

Performance and Diagnostic Technologies

  • Sentry error tracking for crash reporting and diagnostics

Server-Side Logging

  • Timestamp of request
  • IP address of the requesting device
  • Type of API request
  • HTTP response status code
  • Approximate data volume of request and response

AI Processing Identifiers

When food photographs or text descriptions are submitted to the Google Gemini API for nutritional analysis, technical request identifiers may be created to track the request and connect the response to the correct user session.

3. Third-Party Tracking by Service Providers

  • Sentry
  • Supabase
  • Google LLC (Gemini API)
  • Upstash
  • Apple Inc. / Google LLC (App Store Platforms)

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not use advertising cookies or advertising SDKs
  • We do not share tracking data with advertising networks or data brokers
  • We do not use cross-application tracking technologies to monitor your activity outside the app

5. Your Choices and Controls

  • Logging out invalidates your authentication session token
  • Deleting your account removes personal information subject to legal retention obligations
  • You may contact us regarding diagnostic reporting concerns

6. Data Retention for Tracking Technologies

  • Authentication session tokens: expire upon logout or after 1 hour of inactivity, whichever is earlier
  • Upstash cache keys: expire automatically after a short period
  • Sentry diagnostic reports: retained according to configured retention settings
  • Server access logs: retained according to backend provider settings

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about the tracking technologies we use, please contact us at caloriesbunnysupport@gmail.com.
